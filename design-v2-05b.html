FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first class

Absolutely not. Most of our students start with zero experience. Our programs are designed to take you from complete beginner to confident practitioner at your own pace. Our coaches will work with you one-on-one to make sure you're comfortable.

For your first class, comfortable athletic clothing is all you need — shorts or sweats and a t-shirt work great. For BJJ classes, we have loaner gis available. No shoes are needed on the mat.

Our kids programs start at age 5. Ninja Kids and Kids Jiu-Jitsu are designed for ages 5-12. Adult programs are open to ages 13 and up. There is no upper age limit — we have students in their 50s and 60s training regularly.

Yes! You don't need to be fit to start — you start to get fit. Our classes are scalable, meaning every drill and exercise can be adjusted to your current fitness level. Many of our students came to us specifically to get in shape.

Safety is our top priority. All sparring is controlled, protective gear is required, and our instructors closely supervise every session. Beginners start with fundamentals and don't spar until they're ready. Our injury rate is very low.

Absolutely! Your first class is completely free with no obligation. Come try any of our programs and see if it's the right fit for you. We want you to be 100% confident before you commit.

Yes, private lessons are available with both our kickboxing and BJJ instructors. These are great for accelerating your learning or preparing for competition. Ask at the front desk for scheduling and pricing.
