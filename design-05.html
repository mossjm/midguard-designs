Meet Your Coaches

Led by The Best

Decades of combined experience. Real fight credentials. A shared passion for developing champions.

Josh Pinney

Owner & Head Kickboxing Instructor

Training since 2002. Black Belt (I.K.C.A, 2007). BJJ Purple Belt. US Army veteran who trained with Dan Henderson, Gray Maynard, and Mark Munoz. Josh has coached multiple state and national champions, bringing elite-level knowledge to every class.

Robert Tahtinen

Head BJJ Instructor — Nibiru BJJ

Black Belt (TAC Team, 2021). Training since 2011 after 15 years of wrestling. Retired MMA fighter with four title belts and a King of the Cage victory. Winner of IBJJF and Arizona State tournaments, Robert brings championship-level grappling expertise to the mat.
