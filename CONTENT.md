## Schedule
### Kids Classes (5:00 PM - 5:45 PM)
**Monday & Wednesday - Kids Jiu-jitsu**
45-minute class introducing kids to fundamentals of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a safe, fun, structured environment. No-gi techniques: takedowns, guard movements, escapes, basic submissions. Develops balance, coordination, confidence.

**Tuesday & Thursday - Ninja Kids**
Fun, high-energy program building confidence, coordination, strength. Basics of jiu-jitsu through playful movements, gymnastics-inspired skills (rolls, balance, agility), age-appropriate calisthenics.
