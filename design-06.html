Meet Your Coaches

Friendly Faces, World-Class Skills

Our coaches combine decades of experience with a genuine love for teaching and mentoring.

Josh Pinney

Owner & Head Kickboxing Instructor

Training since 2002. Black Belt (I.K.C.A, 2007) and BJJ Purple Belt. A US Army veteran who trained with Dan Henderson, Gray Maynard, and Mark Munoz. Josh has coached multiple state and national champions and brings a welcoming, encouraging teaching style to every class.

Robert Tahtinen

Head BJJ Instructor — Nibiru BJJ

Black Belt (TAC Team, 2021). Training since 2011 after 15 years of wrestling. A retired MMA fighter with four title belts and a King of the Cage victory. Robert is known for his patient, detailed instruction and his ability to make every student feel at home on the mat.
